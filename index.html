About SW.LIGHTS

SW.LIGHTS is an Irish handcrafted lighting brand based in Co. Louth, specialising in bold, custom-made timber and steel lighting for both domestic and commercial spaces.

Every piece is designed and built by hand using locally sourced timber, forged steel and industrial-grade components. No mass production. No shortcuts. Just honest materials and solid craftsmanship.

Our lights are built for homes, bars, cafés, restaurants, workshops and feature interiors. Whether it's a statement table lamp, a wall light, or a full commercial fit-out, each piece is made to order and tailored to your space.
